Our history

For centuries, the area that is now Bangladesh was part of the Bengal region of India. It was ruled by the same empires that ruled central India, from the Maurya (321–184 BCE) to the Mughal (CE 1526–1858). When the British took control of the region and created their Raj in India (1858–1947), Bangladesh was included.
